Fixing and development

Whatever the nature of our customers’ problems, we are able to enter into their field and find a way to use technology to improve their workflow, at the same time managing not to create friction/difficulties between humans and computers.

Consulting
for your projects

If you’re about to start a new project and you want to get off on the right foot, we can help you prepare the groundwork for a successful product. Working alongside your team, we choose the right technologies, define the procedures and plan the best architecture for your situation. Alternatively, if your project is almost complete and you want to go live without any problems, we can review your code to identify potential performance, security or maintenance issues.

Teaching and coaching

If your team needs updating on the topic of software development and new technologies available, we can organise tailor-made courses for you. You choose the topic, length and format. We’ll take care of the content, making it our responsibility to provide stimulating lessons with teachers that have real experience in working with the tools they teach.
